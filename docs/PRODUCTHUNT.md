## 常见问题准备

**Q: Is this like PDF Expert?**
A: Feature-wise similar, but Lumen is 100% free and open source. No subscription, no account needed.

**Q: What about Skim?**
A: Skim is X11-based and feels dated. Lumen is built with modern SwiftUI and native macOS conventions.

**Q: Data privacy?**
A: All data stays on your device. No cloud sync, no account, no telemetry.
